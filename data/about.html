On behalf of the Anita Borg Institute, I welcome you to the twelfth Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing. The Anita Borg Institute is building a global community of technical women and men from industry, academia, and government who are committed to positively transform the global technology workplace to one that is welcoming to women. I encourage all of our attendees to engage in this community, to connect with other women, to explore new technologies, and to understand how to advance your career.
